About Me

Welcome! I’m Anushree Sanjai, a passionate designer currently pursuing a degree in Experience Design with a minor in Psychology at FLAME University, Pune. My work centers around creating thoughtful, user-centered experiences that combine aesthetic appeal with functional, intuitive design.

I’m driven to create a classic, timeless brand identity that appeals to high-end clients and established companies. This journey is about learning, growing, and challenging myself to become not only a skilled designer but also an entrepreneur. In my work, I’m inspired by vintage aesthetics and the timeless appeal of classic design, which I aim to bring into both digital and physical experiences.

Beyond design, I’m invested in personal growth, fitness, and sharing insights through my future podcast, where I’ll invite guests to discuss life skills and self-development. This is a space where I explore and share my creative journey, so I invite you to follow along as I continue to evolve and learn in this field. Thank you for visiting, and I hope you enjoy browsing my portfolio!
